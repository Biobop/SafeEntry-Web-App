Check-In

To check in, students will scan a QR code, which will bring them to this website, with the address /checkin/(venue code)/(seat number).

Click here for the link for seat 01 in Library. (LIB for Library, ICO for iCodeLab, SHL for Senior High Lounge and JHL for Junior High Lounge)

Check-Out

The checkout page will only appear after someone has checked in. After checking in, the checkout page will only consist of one button, "check out". Students click this button to check out.

Each checkout page for each seat in every venue is a unique sha256 string, preventing students from bruteforcing through all the urls and checking out for others.

If the seat has not been checked in, the check-out url for that seat will not work.
